Medicos del Mundo's Humanitarian Aid along the Ukrainian Conflict Line

MdM Spain aims to deliver emergency and long-term sustainable healthcare solutions to vulnerable people in Syria, Yemen, Palestine, and Ukraine. MdM has two mobile clinics located in Popsana and Stanytsyia Luhansk. MdM mobile clinics are modalities to improve access to primary care. The mobile clinics provide free primary care consultation and medication. MdM provides multi-purpose cash to conflict affected beneficiaries for secondary and tertiary level care.

The Conflict in Ukraine

The armed conflict resulted in the division of Ukraine into two areas: Government Controlled Area (GCA) and Non-Government Controalled Area (NGCA) with limited cross points. 500-kilometer Line of Contact between the GCA and NGCA areas has only three pedestrian border crossing points with only one point with medical clinic The resources for the conflict have become scare. Due to the conflict now in its fith year, many humanitarian organizations are moving on to other complex conflict areas.
